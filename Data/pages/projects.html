Stolen Timelines

Stolen Timelines is a fast paced platformer, where you play as a thief from the future, stealing artefacts from the past in order to meet the gang quota.

Taking from a wide range of games like Lethal Company and Battle Block Theatre, we developed the game with the focus on being easy to pick up and fast paced gameplay.

You will find yourself in Victorian London, locating and stealing all kinds of artefacts. All while dodging traps such as the Iron Maiden that traps and impales you with sharp spikes, Brazen Bull rooms which will slowly cook you alive and deadly rats running about the rooms.
Time is not your friend, you only have two minutes to get in and extract before being left behind.

Bar Fight

This project was completed during my 3rd year at Abertay during the Global Game Jam 2024. The theme being: Make Me Laugh.

Bar Fight is a first-person gaming experience that combines mayhem with a touch of irreverent humour.

Immerse yourself in this wild, low-poly universe where you'll navigate through a rowdy bar setting, testing your skills in the art of the brawl.

Networking with FishNet

This project explored how games handle online play, focusing on which network architecture is best for small-scale games and development and which API best fits the network type.

The outcome consits of a basic, playable prototype in Unity.

Within the game players can move, shoot and kill other players that have joined their server.
